# Cloud Masking A/B Testing Report

## Objective
To compare the performance of 3 cloud masking algorithms over the chronic cloud-cover in Arunachal Pradesh.

## Strategies Evaluated
1. **Control (C1)**: Quality Mosaic via Landsat 8 QA_PIXEL bitmask and custom cloud score logic (derived from user legacy code).
2. **Experimental (C2)**: Sentinel-2 Harmonized joining with `s2cloudless` probabilties < 50% mask and median compositing.
3. **Experimental (C3)**: Sentinel-2 Harmonized joining with Cloud Score Plus (CS+) thresholds >= 0.6 and median compositing.

## Methodology
- **Metric**: Standard Deviation of NDVI over stable old-growth forest regions. (Lower SD assumes fewer unmasked clouds/shadows).
